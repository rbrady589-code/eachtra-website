Who we are

We tell tales
of the
extraordinary.

In Irish, eachtra means the tale of an extraordinary event — the kind of story that changes the shape of everything that follows it. That is what we believe every business deserves. Not a campaign. A story worth telling.

We work with artists, event brands, and companies whose work carries real weight in the world. We map their audience with the precision of a cartographer and the instinct of someone who has sat in the crowd, danced at the event, believed in the product. We know these worlds because we live in them.

Every client receives the full system — the research, the creative, the distribution, the follow-through. You devote yourself to the work. We devote ourselves to the rest.
